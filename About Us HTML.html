About Us

"AgriConnect - Farming management system" ,The farming management system is an e-commerce website and mobile application that connects farmers directly with consumers, selling fresh fruits and vegetables online that allows customers to shop for a wide variety of fresh produce from their computers or smartphones. The application displays pictures and descriptions of the products, and customers can add items to their virtual shopping cart and then pay for them securely. The fruits and vegetables are then delivered to the customer's doorstep, ensuring convenience and freshness. The web/mobile application also offers features like discounts, customer reviews, and helpful information about the products. It's a convenient way for consumers to access farm-fresh produce while also supporting farmers in their communities and supporting local agriculture.
